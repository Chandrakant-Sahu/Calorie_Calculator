What is BMR ?

Basal Metabolic Rate is the number of calories required to keep your body functioning at rest. BMR is also known as your body’s metabolism; therefore, any increase to your metabolic weight, such as exercise, will increase your BMR. To get your BMR, simply input your height, gender, age and weight. Once you’ve determined your BMR, you can begin to monitor how many calories a day you need to maintain or lose weight.
